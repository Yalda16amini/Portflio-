About me

I am Yalda, a multidisciplinary artist from Afghanistan with a deep-rooted passion for visual storytelling and cultural expression through fine arts. My artistic journey spans several mediums including miniature painting, charcoal drawing, oil painting, watercolor, ink techniques, and mixed media.

I have participated in several national and international art exhibitions. Most notably, I was honored with top recognition at an international exhibition in Germany, where my artwork was showcased across multiple cities and received both first and seventh place distinctions.
